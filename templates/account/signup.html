BUFIA Terms & Conditions

By creating an account, you confirm that you are an authorized member of Bawayan United Farmers Irrigations Incorporated (BUFIA) and agree to abide by the following rules:

Use of the System

  • Account access is provided to support BUFIA operations such as machine reservations, maintenance tracking, irrigation scheduling, and farmer communications.
  • You must provide accurate profile and membership details. Any change in contact information should be updated promptly.
  • Sharing login credentials or impersonating another member is strictly prohibited.

Data Responsibility

  • Information you submit, including rental forms, reports, and photos, must be truthful and authorized for BUFIA use.
  • You acknowledge that BUFIA administrators may audit, retain, or remove data that violates policies or legal requirements.
  • Downloaded documents or reports should be stored securely to protect member privacy.

Community Conduct

  • Respect other members when communicating through the platform.
  • Use equipment responsibly and follow the schedules approved within the system.
  • Report technical issues, safety concerns, or suspected misuse to BUFIA officers immediately.

BUFIA may suspend or terminate accounts that violate these conditions or applicable laws.
