Service Flow

  1. Farmer submits the dryer request and chooses Until Dried or By Hour.
  2. BUFIA staff reviews the request and checks availability.
  3. After service, BUFIA staff records the final billing details. {% if is_per_sack_pricing %} This uses the admin-confirmed final sacks multiplied by the saved sack rate. {% else %} This uses the recorded drying hours multiplied by the dryer rate. {% endif %}
  4. The renter chooses the payment method and completes payment, or staff confirms an over-the-counter payment.
  5. Once payment is confirmed, BUFIA staff marks the request as completed.
